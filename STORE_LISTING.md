Discover the best of Israel — beautifully.

Israel Attractions is your trendy travel companion for exploring the Holy Land. From the Old City of Jerusalem to the floating waters of the Dead Sea, from Tel Aviv's beaches to the coral reefs of Eilat, find what to see, plan your route, and share it with friends.

🗺️ EXPLORE
• 30+ curated attractions across Israel
• Beautiful interactive map with color-coded regions
• Filter by region, category (nature, history, religious, beach, museum…), family-friendly, free entry, or "open right now"
• "Near me" mode — sorts attractions by distance from your location
• Stunning photos for every place
• Hours, prices, and details in 3 languages

⭐ TRIP PLANNER
• Build a custom itinerary by tapping ★ on any attraction
• See your route on the map with distance + driving time
• Auto-split your trip into 1–5 days
• Reorder stops with a tap
• Share your trip via link — anyone can import it

💬 GROUP CHAT
• Plan trips together in WhatsApp-style group chats
• Share attractions and trips directly into chats
• Send photos and voice notes
• React to messages with emojis
• Reply, edit, delete
• Real-time typing indicators and online presence
• Search messages, day separators, unread counts

🌐 THREE LANGUAGES
• English, Hebrew (RTL), and Arabic (RTL) — switch on the fly

🎨 STUNNING DESIGN
• Modern glassmorphism UI
• Gradient pins, smooth animations
• Dark theme that's easy on the eyes
• Optimized for both phones and tablets

📸 NATIVE FEATURES
• Take photos with your camera and share them in chat
• Use your GPS to find attractions near you
• Native share sheet to send trips to anyone

Whether you're planning your first visit or you're a local rediscovering your country — Israel Attractions makes it fun.

Free, no ads, no account required.
