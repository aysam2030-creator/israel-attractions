Privacy Policy

Israel Attractions · Last updated: April 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Israel Attractions mobile application ("the App", "we", "our") handles information.

What we don't collect

We do not:

  • Collect personal information from you
  • Require account registration
  • Track your activity across other apps or websites
  • Sell or share your data with third parties for advertising
  • Use third-party analytics SDKs

What's stored on your device

The App stores the following locally on your device only, never transmitted to us:

  • Your selected language preference
  • Your saved trip (favorited attractions and order)
  • Your chat messages, groups, and identity selection
  • Photos and voice recordings you create within chat

You can clear all of this at any time by uninstalling the App or clearing its data in your device settings.

Permissions we ask for

The App may ask for these permissions, only when you use the related feature:

PermissionWhy
CameraTo take photos to share inside group chats
Photo LibraryTo pick existing photos to share inside chats
MicrophoneTo record voice notes inside chats
LocationTo find attractions near your current location ("Near me" feature)

You can deny or revoke any of these in your device settings without breaking the rest of the App.

Network requests

The App makes network requests to:

  • OpenStreetMap tile servers (*.tile.openstreetmap.org) to render the map
  • OSRM routing service (router.project-osrm.org) to calculate driving distance and time between trip stops
  • Unsplash (images.unsplash.com) to load attraction photos
  • Google Maps — only when you tap "Open in Google Maps" on an attraction

These services may log standard request metadata (IP address, user agent) under their own privacy policies. We do not send them any personal information.

Children's privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. The content (Israeli tourist attractions) is appropriate for all ages.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we'll update the "Last updated" date and post a notice in the App.
